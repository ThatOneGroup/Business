Virtual Reality

The first iteration of VR (Virtual Reality) was made in 1960, by Morton Heilig. This version was called the Telesphere Mask, which was the first ever HMD (Head Mounted Display). Although it is not an interactive device like what has been a trend recently, it provided a 3D stereoscopic wide vision, and stereo sound.
